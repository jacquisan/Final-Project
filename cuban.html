Recipes: Omi's Cuban Style Sandwich

The Cuban Style Sandwich is a popular food option in Cuba but also used for many coffee shops. There have been many versions of this sandwich due to its meat options. In this recipe we'll explore a version made by us! So lets get into it.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of ciabatta bread
  • Olive Oil
  • Ham
  • 1/2 pound of Chicken Breast Thin Sliced
  • Paprika
  • Lemmon Pepper seasoning
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced jalapeños
  • Spinach
  • Mustard
  • Mayo

Optional, but highly recommended:

  • Mexican Queso Fresco
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocado

Tools Needed

  • Skillet Pan
  • Small griddle
  • Cutting Board & Knives

Good to Know

  • Prep Time:10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time:40-50 minutes
  • Yields: 2 servings

Steps

Meat Preparation

  1. You want to begin with the meats! as you preheat the skillet pan for 10 minutes in medium temperature cut up slices of ham (if not sliced) and cut up thin slices of Chicken breasts.When pan is hot enough, pour half a cup of olive oil.
  2. Cover the cut up chicken breast slices with paprika and lemon pepper seasoning. As soon as its fully covered put them on the skillet plan and let it cook!rotate as needed until fully cooked.

Bread

  1. In your griddle, heat it up using medium temperature.
  2. When hot enough put 1/2 of butter on the griddle. Spread evenly across the griddle
  3. Cut the ciabatta bread in half making it into two long slices
  4. Place face down where the inside of the bread is on the griddle first
  5. Flip the slices!
  6. You will be done once both sides are crispy, put it to the side

Preparation

With all the meats and bread properly cooked, we now put everything together.Lay the two toasted pieces of bread with the inside facing up. Spread mustard and mayo. Layer some sliced tomatoes, spinach, pickles and jalapeños.Add the chicken and ham on the other slice of ciabatta. Put it all together and enjoy!!
