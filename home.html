E-Z Chef

The one thing AI can't do for college students:
Cook.

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

EZ Chef prioritizes helping college students with different recipes that can help them make cooking easy despite their busy schedule, limited supplies and intentions with their food!

Our Goals

As students and chefs we hope too...

Provide tasty recipes

All our dishes use spices and different ingredients or same ones but never taste the same and always keep you smiling.

Diversify your palete

We have a section full of ethnic recipes but before you panic, all of them are easy to make and don't require extra hands.

Use up all your ingredients

Sometimes we dont know what to do with two random ingredients but with EZ Chef we hope to use up all those ingredients to make delicious food.

Make sure you eat

Eat! our goal isn't to make you the best chef on campus but simply to give you options and make sure you are eating.
