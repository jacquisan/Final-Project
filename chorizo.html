Recipes: Chorizo Quesadilla

Chorizo Quesadilla is a popular food option in Mexico. With new types of Chorizo; plant based beef, pork, chicken This recipe can be used on any of your liking. In this recipe we'll explore a version made by us! So lets get into it.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Olive Oil
  • 3 cups Chorizo of any kind (here we use vegan chorizo)
  • 6 slices of cheese (any kind, here we are using swiss)
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced jalapeños
  • Sliced Avocado

Optional, but highly recommended:

  • Mexican Queso Fresco to replace Swiss
  • Sour Cream

Tools Needed

  • Skillet Pan
  • Small griddle
  • Cutting Board & Knives

Good to Know

  • Prep Time:10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 30-40 minutes
  • Yields: 3 quesadillas

Steps

Meat Preparation

  1. You want to begin with the chorizo! as you preheat the skillet pan for 8 minutes in medium temperature cut up your jalapeños and tomatoes (if not sliced). When pan is hot enough, pour 2/3 spoon of olive oil.
  2. As soon as skillet is hot put in all the chorizo and let it sit with the top on for 3 minutes. Check again and move it all around. Once choizo looks like its cooking well put in all the cut up jalapeños and tomatoes in the skillet as well. After about 10 minutes everything should be cooked enough.
  3. Turn off stove and let the chorizo sit while making the quesadillas

Quesadilla

  1. In your griddle, heat it up using medium temperature for 5 minutes. When hot enough put 1/2 of butter on the griddle. Spread evenly across the griddle
  2. Put three tortillas on it let them heat up both sides for about 3 minutes each side. When it begins to change in to a more darker color grab your 2 slices of swiss cheese for each tortilla.
  3. Keep flipping the tortilla on both sides until cheese begins to melt. Once you notice the cheese melting grab your chorizo and put a good amount on each of the quesadillas.
  4. You will be done once both sides are crispy and cheese has fully melted with the chorizo.

Preparation

At this point everything is done but to add more flavor in it... grab your slices of avocado and pu at least two in each quesadilla. Enjoy!
