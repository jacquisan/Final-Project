recipes: stir-fry

asian stir-fry is easy to prepare and cook. many of these ingredients are substitutable too, including the chicken. you can use what ever vegetables you like, this is a version made by us that brings the most nutrician for your buck.


ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1.5 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons mushroom soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Tools Needed

  • Skillet Pan
  • Cutting Board & Knives

Good to Know

  • Prep Time:10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 30-40 minutes
  • Yields: 1 serving

steps

step 1

heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and garlic, stir-fry until chicken is browned, 5 to 8 minutes.

step 2

stir in broccoli, bell pepper, carrots, celery, zucchini, green onions, and salt for 4 to 8 minutes.

step3

stir water, soy sauce, and cornstarch together in a small bowl; pour into the wok. Cook and stir until sauce is thickened, 1 to 2 minutes.
